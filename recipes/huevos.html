Huevos de/a la Mexicana

Description

Delicious Mexican style scrambled eggs.

Best breakfast. Great with some tea, or even coffee.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Jalepeno
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Oil
  • Tortilla
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Chop veggies
  2. Add onions to pan
  3. Add jalepenos after the onions have begun to sweat. Do NOT add jalepenos first unless you don't mind macing yourself.
  4. Add tomatoes, let boil down
  5. Add egg
  6. While this scrambles up, have tortillas cooking on another burner
  7. Eat the eggs with the tortillas!
